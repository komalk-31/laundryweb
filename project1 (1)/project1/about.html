We Are locally Owned

We Are passionate

Thank you for taking the time to visit our site. cleanza is a locally owned and family owned laundry business. We have a large family and all love clean clothes, but don’t want to spend all of our free time keeping them clean. That’s why our mission is all about giving you back precious time to do things you love. When you use our affordable wash and fold service, we are able to virtually eliminate this chore from your life. If you prefer to do your own laundry or wash and fold service doesn’t currently fit in your budget, we can still dramatically reduce the time you spend doing laundry. Our stores have machines of all sizes, including many jumbo machines that wash up to 10 loads of laundry at once. Why spend all day running cycle after cycle of laundry when you can wash your entire family’s laundry all in about an hour?

The even better news is that you can make that hour enjoyable or productive by catching your favorite show or the game on one of our flat screen TV’s, doing work on your laptop, catching up with friends and family on your smartphone, or surfing the web on your tablet with our complimentary WiFi. We can also keep you from getting hungry at our store with our two vending machines that provide a wide assortment of snacks, candy, and ice cold water and soft drinks.

Let Us Handle It

We also understand that your time is precious, and you may not want or be able to take that hour or so to do your laundry. No worries! We’ve got you covered for that as well. Simply drop off your clothes, and we’ll take care of the rest. Our friendly and skilled team members will listen to your special requests and deliver what you ask every time. They’ll ask what detergent and fabric softener you prefer, what temperature you’d like your clothes washed in, and any other special fabric handling needs you have. And you only need to tell us once, because will capture all of your laundering requests in our customer database for your next visit. Best yet, we’ll take care of all of this at a very modest price.
